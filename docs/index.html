Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026-02-25

1. Information We Collect

Analytics Data
We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data such as hymn views, category views, audio interactions, search usage, and settings changes. This data does not personally identify you.

Crash Data
We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports including device type, iOS version, and app version for stability improvements.

Bible API Usage
Scripture references are retrieved via external Bible APIs such as YouVersion. These services operate under their own privacy policies.

2. Data Storage

User preferences (theme, font size, etc.) are stored locally on your device. Resonate does not require user accounts and does not collect names, emails, or personal identifiers.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • YouVersion Bible API

4. Advertising

Resonate does not display advertisements and does not use advertising identifiers.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
